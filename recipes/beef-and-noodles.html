Southern Slow Cooked Beef and Noodles

Beef and Noodles

Someing nice and delicious to drop all in one pot and let it go.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef for stew
  • 1 10 1/2 oz can Campbell's condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 10 1/2 oz can Campbell's condensed french onion soup
  • 12 oz uncooked medium egg noodles

Instructions

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper. Place the beef into a 3 1/2-quart slow cooker. Stir the soups in a small bowl. Pour the soup mixture over the beef.
  2. Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours or until the beef is fork-tender. Cook and drain the noodles according to the package directions during the last 20 minutes of the beef cooking time.
  3. Season the beef mixture to taste. Serve over the noodles.
