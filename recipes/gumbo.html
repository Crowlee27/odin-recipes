Slap Yo Mama Good Gumbo

Chicken, Sausage, Shrimp Gumbo.

This amazing Gumbo and packed with classic Louisiana flavor for the whole house. Whatever you do, do NOT burn the roux.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thigh (skinless)
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 8 oz smoked sausage
  • 1/4 unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 medium onion (diced)
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 medium green bell pepper (diced)
  • 1 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1/2 lb crab legs
  • 1 tbsp creole seasoning
  • 1 tbsp chicken bouillon powder
  • 1/2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp thyme (fresh or dried)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 14 oz can tomatoes (chopped)
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 1 lb shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 tbsp gumbo file
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parsley (chopped)

Steps

  1. Lightly season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the oil over medium heat in a heavy-bottomed Dutch oven. Then cook the chicken until browned on both sides, remove, and set aside. Add the sausage and brown, and then remove. Set aside.
  3. Melt the butter and oil in a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed saucepan, then add the flour and stir until smooth.
  4. Cook on medium heat, stirring continuously, for about 20-30 minutes or until it turns a rich dark brown color - just like chocolate. Don't walk away from the stove during this process, or it could burn.
  5. When you have achieved your desired color, remove the Dutch oven from the stove and let it cool.
  6. Return the Dutch oven to the stove. Add the onion, garlic, green pepper, and celery, and cook for 8-10 minutes-stirring frequently.
  7. Then add chicken, sausage, crab legs, Creole seasoning, chicken bouillon or cubes, paprika, thyme, bay leaves, and let it cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Follow with the canned tomatoes and about 6 cups of chicken stock, then bring it all to a boil and simmer it for 45-50 minutes. After that, add the shrimp and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  9. Stir in file powder, green onions, and parsley. Adjust the soup's thickness and flavor with broth or water and salt.
